title: "Cheese and tomato tartlets"
tags: Dairy, Appetiser
---

cookTime: ~10 minutes
recipeYield: 10 portions

ingredients:
- 60g Cream cheese
- 30ml tomato sauce
- 30ml Cream
- paprika
- 10 tartlet cases
- 250ml aspic jelly

directions:
- Combine cheese, tomato sauce, cream and paprika in mixer.
- Pipe into tartlet cases - glaze with aspic.
