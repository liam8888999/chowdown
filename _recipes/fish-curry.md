Best served with white rice

cookTime: 30 min
recipeYield:5

## Ingredients
- 3T neutral oil
- 1 onion
- 1 red onion
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 4cm ginger
- 1 red pepper
- 1t curry powder
- 250g green beans
- 4 tomatoes
- 200ml fish broth
- 200ml coconut milk
- 400g fish fillet (white fish)

## Directions
- Boil green beans for 10 minutes
- Cut onions, tomato, garlic and fish.
- Heat oil on a wok and saut√© the onion and garlic
- Add beans, tomato, broth and spices and boil for 6 minutes
- Add coconut milk, fish and pepper (whole) and boil for an additional 6 minutes.
