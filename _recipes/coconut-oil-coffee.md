title: "Coconut Oil Coffee"
tags: drink, sweet, breakfast
---

recipeYield: 1

ingredients:
- 1 cup hot coffee
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp unsalted butter

directions:
- Blend coffee, coconut oil, and butter together in a blender until oil and butter are melted and coffee is frothy.
